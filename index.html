Privacy Policy

Effective date: 20 April 2025  ·  Last updated: 20 April 2025

Linkly is a personal link-saving app. Your links live on your device. No account is required, no data is sold, and no analytics are collected.

1. What data Linkly stores

All data you enter is stored locally on your device in a private SQLite database:

  • URLs, titles, descriptions, and thumbnail images you save
  • Categories and tags you create
  • Reminder dates and times you set

This data never leaves your device unless you choose to back it up to your own Google Drive (see section 3).

2. Data Linkly fetches automatically

When you save a link, Linkly may make two outbound requests to improve your experience:

  • Open Graph metadata — Linkly fetches the page you are saving to read its title, description, and preview image. This is a standard HTTP request identical to a browser visiting the page.
  • Site favicon — Linkly loads a small icon for the domain via Google's public favicon service (https://www.google.com/s2/favicons). Google's own privacy policy applies to this request.

No personal information is sent in these requests beyond the URL itself.

3. Optional Google Drive backup

You may connect your Google account to back up and restore your links. If you do:

  • Linkly requests access only to its own private app-data folder in Google Drive (drive.appdata scope). This folder is invisible to you and to other apps — it cannot be browsed in Google Drive.
  • Your links are written as a single JSON file in that private folder. Nothing else is read from or written to your Google Drive.
  • Google Sign-In is handled entirely by Google Play Services on your device. Linkly never sees your Google password.
  • You can disconnect Google Drive at any time from within the app, or revoke access via myaccount.google.com/permissions.

4. Reminders and notifications

Reminders are scheduled locally using Android's AlarmManager. No reminder data is transmitted to any server. Notifications are displayed entirely on your device.

5. Data Linkly does NOT collect

  • No account registration or sign-up is required to use the app
  • No analytics or crash-reporting SDKs are included
  • No advertising networks or third-party trackers
  • No location, contacts, camera, microphone, or any sensor data

6. Data retention and deletion

All your data is stored on your device. You can delete individual links at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. If you have a Google Drive backup, delete it by revoking access at myaccount.google.com/permissions or by manually deleting the backup file from your Drive app-data folder.

7. Children's privacy

Linkly is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
